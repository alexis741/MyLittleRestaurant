About Us

My Little Restaurant first opened its doors on October 11, 2019, celebrating its official grand opening just 8 days later. As a Latinx-owned business, we bring authentic Mexican cuisine in Selah, Washington with a Jaliscan taste. Nereyda Capi (middle right) and her 20+ years of experience inspired her homemade recipes to take on the small town restaurant experience, big on flavors with our daily specials and other traditional classics. Breakfast, lunch, or dinner, we are open 7 days a week: burritos, ceviche, pozole, quesadillas, tacos, and more!
